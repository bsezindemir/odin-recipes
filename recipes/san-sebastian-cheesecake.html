San Sebastian Cheesecake

This San Sebastian Cheesecake is a traditional Spanish Basque cheesecake. Perfectly light, creamy and smooth, baked to caramelized perfection! Easy to make and crustless!

INGREDIENTS

  • 800 grams of softened cream cheese
  • 3 whole eggs + 1 egg white
  • 315 grams of powdered sugar
  • 600 ml of heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla extract

STEPS

  1. Preheat your oven at 250°C.
  2. Add 800 grams of softened cream cheese in a mixing bowl
  3. In a separate bowl beat sugar and eggs on high speed for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Beat softened cream cheese on high speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Add heavy cream (room temperature) on top of the cream cheese and whisk until the mixture is smooth
  6. Add flour, cornstarch and vanilla extract, then whisk until no lumps remain.
  7. Add and gently fold in the egg mixture.
  8. Grease and line an 8 inch round pan with greaseproof paper.
  9. Pour the mixture in, bake at 250°C for 10 minutes, lower the temperature to 200°C and bake 20 more minutes.
  10. Let it cool to room temperature and chill overnight before serving.
